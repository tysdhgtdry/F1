Fernando Alonso

About Fernando Alonso

Fernando Alonso is a 42 year old racer from Spain and is the oldest active racer. He is a 2 time world champion and has won 32 Grand Prixs. He has raced for many teams like Minardi, Renault, Mclaren, Ferrari, Alphine, and Aston Martin currently. Although he hasn't won a race since 2013, He has stayed a cosistent driver and has many podiums. Even at age old age of 42 for an athelete, he recently extended his contract and plans to race as long as he feels.
